Could Touch-Typing Be Bad For You?

Why people who don’t touch-type, type with just two fingers, don’t seem to get any ergonomics related problems? Perhaps touch-typing is the root of the problems. Do not touchtype. Save money on the fancy keyboard. You will be typing less. Staring at the screen less. And perhaps thinking a little more. I type this on my 4th mechanical keyboard.
